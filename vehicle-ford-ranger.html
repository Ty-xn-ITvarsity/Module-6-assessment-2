The Ford Ranger Wildtrak offers premium features and powerful performance that sets it apart from the competition. With its robust 3.2L turbo diesel engine delivering 200HP and 470Nm of torque, this bakkie is engineered for both demanding work tasks and comfortable leisure driving.

This particular unit comes with luxurious leather appointments, advanced navigation system, and a comprehensive suite of safety features including adaptive cruise control and lane keeping assist.
